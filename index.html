Original Branded Content

Pujo Micro-Dramas

Short-format fictional festive micro-dramas rooted in relatable Bengali Pujo incidents. Instead of overt fashion storytelling, Ethnix becomes part of emotionally memorable festive moments where fashion is part of emotional confidence before stepping out.

The Last-Minute Ashtami Plan

A young woman learns her childhood friend is returning to Kolkata for Pujo after years. The emotional chaos of deciding what to wear, last-minute family reactions, and festive anticipation naturally brings Ethnix into the story.

Baba's First Proper Pujo

A daughter secretly plans to make her overworked father finally enjoy Pujo properly again — beginning with gifting him a festive Ethnix look before their family outing.

The Family Photo Crisis

During a chaotic family gathering before pandal hopping, everyone realises they've accidentally colour-coordinated their festive outfits. A cheerful comedy-of-errors rooted in Bengali family behaviour.
